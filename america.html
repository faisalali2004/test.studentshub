Top Universities in America

  1. Harvard University

    Located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, Harvard is a private Ivy League research university that is widely considered one of the most prestigious universities in the world. It has a strong focus on undergraduate education, as well as graduate and professional programs.

  2. Stanford University

    Located in Stanford, California, Stanford is a private research university that is known for its excellence in academics, research, and athletics. It has produced many successful entrepreneurs and business leaders, and has a strong focus on interdisciplinary collaboration.

  3. Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)

    Located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, MIT is a private research university that is known for its focus on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) education. It has produced many successful inventors and entrepreneurs, and has a strong culture of innovation and collaboration.

  4. California Institute of Technology (Caltech)

    Located in Pasadena, California, Caltech is a private research university that is known for its excellence in science and engineering education. It has a strong focus on undergraduate education, and is home to many groundbreaking scientific discoveries and inventions.

  5. University of Chicago

    Located in Chicago, Illinois, UChicago is a private research university that is known for its rigorous academics and intellectual culture. It has produced many Nobel laureates and MacArthur Fellows, and has a strong focus on interdisciplinary research and collaboration.

  6. Columbia University

    Located in New York, New York, Columbia is a private Ivy League research university that is known for its excellence in humanities and social sciences education. It has produced many notable alumni, including three U.S. Presidents, and has a strong focus on interdisciplinary research and collaboration.

  7. Princeton University

    Located in Princeton, New Jersey, Princeton is a private Ivy League research university that is known for its excellence in undergraduate education. It has produced many notable alumni, including two U.S. Presidents, and has a strong focus on interdisciplinary research and collaboration.

  8. Yale University

    Yale University is a private Ivy League research university in New Haven, Connecticut. It was founded in 1701 and has an undergraduate enrollment of approximately 6,000 students. Yale is known for its strong liberal arts program, including its top-ranked law and business schools.

  9. Columbia University

    Columbia University is a private Ivy League research university located in New York City. It was founded in 1754 and has an undergraduate enrollment of approximately 8,000 students. Columbia is known for its strong programs in journalism, business, and engineering.

  10. University of Pennsylvania

    The University of Pennsylvania is a private Ivy League research university located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. It was founded in 1740 and has an undergraduate enrollment of approximately 10,000 students. Penn is known for its strong programs in business, law, and medicine.

  11. Duke University

    Duke University is a private research university located in Durham, North Carolina. It was founded in 1838 and has an undergraduate enrollment of approximately 6,000 students. Duke is known for its strong programs in medicine, law, and public policy.

  12. Brown University

    Brown University is a private Ivy League research university located in Providence, Rhode Island. It was founded in 1764 and has an undergraduate enrollment of approximately 7,000 students. Brown is known for its strong programs in the humanities and social sciences.

  13. Cornell University

    Cornell University is a private Ivy League research university located in Ithaca, New York. It was founded in 1865 and has an undergraduate enrollment of approximately 15,000 students. Cornell is known for its strong programs in engineering, business, and agriculture.

  14. Northwestern University

    Northwestern University is a private research university located in Evanston, Illinois. It was founded in 1851 and has an undergraduate enrollment of approximately 8,000 students. Northwestern is known for its strong programs in journalism, business, and engineering.

  15. Dartmouth College

    Dartmouth College is a private Ivy League research university located in Hanover, New Hampshire. It was founded in 1769 and has an undergraduate enrollment of approximately 4,400 students. Dartmouth is known for its strong undergraduate program and its focus on undergraduate teaching.

  16. Johns Hopkins University

    Johns Hopkins University: A private research university located in Baltimore, Maryland. Johns Hopkins is known for its top-ranked medical school and its strong programs in the natural sciences, engineering, and public health.

  17. University of Notre Dame

    University of Notre Dame: A private Catholic research university located in Notre Dame, Indiana. Notre Dame is known for its strong undergraduate programs in the liberal arts and its highly-regarded graduate programs in business, law, and theology.

  18. University of California, Berkeley (UC Berkeley)

    University of California, Berkeley (UC Berkeley): A public research university located in Berkeley, California. Berkeley is known for its top-ranked programs in engineering, computer science, and the natural sciences, as well as its strong programs in the humanities and social sciences.

  19. University of Michigan-Ann Arbor

    University of Michigan-Ann Arbor: A public research university located in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Michigan is known for its top-ranked programs in business, law, and engineering, as well as its highly-regarded undergraduate and graduate programs in the arts and sciences.

  20. University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA)

    University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA): A public research university located in Los Angeles, California. UCLA is known for its top-ranked programs in the arts and humanities, as well as its strong programs in the social sciences, natural sciences, and engineering.

  21. University of Virginia

    University of Virginia: A public research university located in Charlottesville, Virginia. UVA is known for its strong undergraduate programs in the liberal arts and its highly-regarded graduate programs in business, law, and education.
